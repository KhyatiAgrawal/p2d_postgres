What is PressToDress?

While lawnparties and formals are two of the most looked-forward-to events at Princeton, they can also be two of the most stressful events of the year. Trying to find the perfect outfit that’s affordable, available, and accessible is a daunting task, but it shouldn’t have to be. We present PressToDress, the one-stop mobile-friendly website for Princeton students to buy, rent, and sell dresses. PressToDress users can make some extra money simply by posting info and photos of their clothes that they want to rent or sell. All of these pictures will then appear on the site’s newsfeed, where students can scroll through images of clothes, add anything they like to their list of favorites, and reach out to the seller if they’re interested in purchasing. With PressToDress, you can painlessly find the perfect outfits at the best prices within walking distance, and you’ll never have to worry about what to wear again.

Don’t stress: just Press to Dress.

A couple of pointers before you start:

Filter dresses by size, price, brand, occasion, buy/rent, and keyword!

Add dresses you like to your "Favorites"!

When you want to rent a dress, enter a rental period, and we'll automatically generate and send an email for you!

Be sure to mark a dress as in-use or available manually once it's been taken or returned!
